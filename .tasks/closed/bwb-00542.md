In `internal/repository/caching/caching.go`: when `opts.ClosedOffset > 0`, bypass the in-memory dashboard cache and call the backing repo directly. On success, append the returned closed slice to the persisted-cache snapshot under the existing lock. When `ClosedOffset == 0`, behavior is unchanged (cache hit path). Unit test: hydrate a warm cache with 35 closed; call Dashboard(opts{ClosedOffset:35, ClosedLimit:50}); assert the backing repo received the call; the persisted snapshot now has 85 closed entries. Edge: cache may be invalidated mid-pagination — backing repo's response is authoritative.
