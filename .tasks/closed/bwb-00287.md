Discovered while implementing beads-workbench-44rr.1. summarizeReferences() in compact mode (internal/ui/details/details.go, renderCompact path) renders 'Blocked by: N  Blocks: N  Related: N' but omits the Children count, so an epic with children shows no children in the compact/summary surface (board hover, narrow terminal). Low impact — compact is a summary view. Fix: include len(detail.Children) in the compact summary alongside the other relationship counts.
