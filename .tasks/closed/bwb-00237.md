## Summary

`toLabelOption` in `read_payloads.go:292` calls `strings.TrimSpace`. `LabelCatalog` at `read_gateway.go:720-723` skips entries where `mapped.Name == ""` after trim. Real-bd audit verified bd 1.0.4 can store labels like `"  whitespace-label  "` and `"   "` (all-space) — the gateway correctly trims/skips. No unit test fixture exercises these paths.

## Discovered during

`beads-workbench-r1m3` Read-catalogs audit (parent epic: `beads-workbench-yspw`).

## Fix

Add `labels_whitespace.json` fixture in `internal/gateway/beads/testdata/` with one whitespace-padded entry and one all-space entry. Add a `TestGatewayCatalogStripsWhitespaceLabels` test asserting the all-space entry is excluded from output and the padded entry is trimmed.
