## Description
Combine event-driven invalidation with time-based staleness so the active view stays fresh without turning every surface into a polling loop.

## Instructions
1. Track per-surface dirty/stale metadata and last-refresh timestamps in `internal/app/model.go`.
2. Keep immediate detail reload after successful edit/update/close/comment/status flows, and mark board/search dirty when those writes can invalidate browse data.
3. Refresh only the active surface on eligible focus regain, timer ticks, or view activation when that surface is dirty or stale.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Write flows still reload detail immediately after success
- [ ] Board/search can be marked dirty by writes and refresh on the next eligible active-view refresh
- [ ] Auto-refresh is limited to the active surface rather than hidden background polling of all views
- [ ] Default refresh cadence is low-frequency (about 60 seconds) and test-covered
