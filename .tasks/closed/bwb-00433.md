44rr.2 (Q4) inverted the cursor-vs-subject markers: the movable cursor was given a background highlight (styles.DependencyCursorStyle) and the '›' chevron was reassigned to the loaded subject. The user's mental model — and the rest of the app — is the opposite: the row you MOVE with up/down is the '›' cursor, identical in style everywhere (board, search, metadata all route through styles.SelectionPrefix). When viewing an epic the subject is never in its own Children list, so '›' rendered nowhere and the cursor looked invisible.

Fix: route the movable cursor through issuerow Selected:true (the universal '› ' prefix); reassign the background highlight to the loaded-subject indicator as an interim. Rename the now-inverted Q4 golden files / test funcs and rewrite the doc blocks in details.go and colors.go. Verify in the real TUI per RUNTIME_UI_VERIFICATION.md, not just regenerated goldens.

Subject=background is provisional pending the user's follow-up steps.
