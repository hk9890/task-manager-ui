In load.go:407-408, errorsIsNotExist checks both os.IsNotExist(err) and errors.Is(err, os.ErrNotExist). Since Go 1.16, os.IsNotExist wraps errors.Is(os.ErrNotExist) internally, making the second check redundant. The wrapper function itself can be replaced with errors.Is(err, os.ErrNotExist) at the single call site (line 86), removing 3 lines and an unnecessary indirection.
