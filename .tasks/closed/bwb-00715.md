## Targets

- **Cold-load post-refactor:** within 10% of best-case (≤ 3.8s) -- the new architecture should match because the bd-fan-out is preserved and may even improve with parallelism tuning.
- **Hit rate post-refactor:** at-least best-case (≥ 85%) AND should NOT degrade with navigation. The per-ID invalidation in [[kxci]] is what fully solves this; in this subtask (E2) the typed cache layer doesn't yet have surgical invalidation, but the structural change (Repository layer with explicit Dashboard cache key) should at least not regress.
- **No regression in idle-tick behavior:** whole minutes with zero gateway misses, as seen in `fb6fed78` for the 18:13 minute (0 miss / 45 hit).
