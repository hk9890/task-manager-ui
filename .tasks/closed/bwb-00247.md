Foundational change for parent bug [[9qpm]] (refresh motion indicator). Adds the dim+phase primitive that 9qpm.2 will wire through board/search/details refresh paths.

## Scope

Extend `internal/ui/shared/issuerow/RenderConfig` with two new fields:
- `Dim bool` — when true, apply foreground tint
- `Phase int` — index into `styles.SkeletonShades`

When `Dim==true`, `RenderCompact` applies `styles.SkeletonShades[phase % len(SkeletonShades)]` to the row foreground.

## Selection-conflict rule (normative)

When `Selected==true && Dim==true`:
- Apply the dim shade to the foreground text only.
- Keep the selection background unchanged.
- The selection highlight must remain visually dominant.

## Files

- `internal/ui/shared/issuerow/row.go` (current dim-shade lookup at lines 50-54)
- `internal/ui/shared/issuerow/row_test.go` (add unit tests)
