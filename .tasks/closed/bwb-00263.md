FakeProcessRunner in internal/testing/fakes/process_runner.go is the only fake that lacks a compile-time interface assertion (var _ launcher.ProcessRunner = (*FakeProcessRunner)(nil)). FakeBeadsGateway (line 101), FakeLauncher (line 25), and FakeEditor (line 24) all have these. If the ProcessRunner interface changes, FakeProcessRunner will silently drift and only fail when a consumer test happens to exercise the mismatch. Add the assertion for consistency and early breakage detection.
