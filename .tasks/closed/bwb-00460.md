## Status: Closed — not reproducible (PTY harness artifact)

This bug was filed during a 2026-05-24 PTY-driven exploration and **does not reflect a real defect**. Kept as a historical record so the same false-positive isn't refiled.

## Original Observation

In the embedded fixture session at 160x40, after typing a search query `bwf` and pressing `Enter`, the PTY capture showed:

- Search header still reading `draft`
- Results header reading `5 partial` instead of `5 results`
- Results pane first row: `Results below are stale. Press Enter to search` — sitting on top of the correct fresh results

The original filing hypothesized either an off-by-one on the draft-vs-applied comparison, a missing `appliedQuery` update on submit, or a wiring mistake on the load-state enum.

## Investigation

In tree at the time of the session, a real "stale draft markers persist" bug had **already been fixed** (commit `38caec3` — `fix(search): mark stale results when draft query differs from applied query; document Enter-to-search`). Unit and teatest coverage in `internal/mode/search/` and `internal/ui/search/` verify the draft/applied/stale transitions work correctly.

The PTY capture's observation was a snapshot taken during the brief loading window between Enter-press and result paint, plus the harness's silent key-fall-through bug (znri.8 fix). The "5 partial" and "stale" markers ARE shown briefly before results land — but they clear correctly once the response arrives. The PTY capture's 8-second wait was apparently still inside the transition window in this particular run.

## Resolution

Closed `not-reproducible`. The real fix that closed the actually-broken version of this bug landed in `38caec3` before the exploration session.
