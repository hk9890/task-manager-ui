## Status: Closed — not reproducible (PTY harness artifact)

This bug was filed during a 2026-05-24 PTY-driven exploration and **does not reflect a real defect**. Kept as a historical record so the same false-positive isn't refiled.

## Original Observation

In the embedded fixture session: press `?` (help modal opens) → press `?` again → modal does not close. Only `Enter` and `Space` dismissed the modal.

`docs/user-guide/key-bindings.md` line 11 says `?` is the help toggle, so the filing concluded the binding is one-way.

## Investigation

Unit tests around the help modal — including `internal/app/help_modal_contract_test.go` — show:

- `?` opens the help modal (correctly).
- `?` while the modal is open is correctly forwarded to the shell's `toggle_help` action and dismisses the modal.
- `Esc`, `Enter`, and `Space` also all dismiss the modal correctly.

The original observation was a side-effect of the PTY harness's unknown-key fall-through bug (znri.8 fix) combined with timing issues — the test send may have been delivered as the wrong key or before the modal was fully mounted.

## Resolution

Closed `not-reproducible`. PTY harness fixed in znri.8 prevents recurrence.
