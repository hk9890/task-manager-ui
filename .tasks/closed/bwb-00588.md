## Description
The structured detail inspector reads the issue owner into `IssueSummary.Assignee` when `bd show --json` includes both `owner` and `assignee`. As a result, the right rail displays the creator/owner instead of the actual assignee for real issues.

## Evidence
- Runtime verification against seeded fixture showed `Assignee  : hans.kohlreiter@dynatrace.com` for `bwf-2`, while `bd show bwf-2 --json` reports `assignee: bob` and `owner: hans.kohlreiter@dynatrace.com`.
- Gateway decoding in `internal/gateway/beads/read_gateway.go` currently prefers `owner` before `assignee`.

## Impact
This breaks the epic success criterion that the right rail correctly covers assignee data.
